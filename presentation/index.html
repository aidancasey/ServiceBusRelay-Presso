Pro's

  • enables evolutionary architectures
  • security - shield your on premise service
  • loose coupling
  • scalability
  • cheap

Con's

  • firewall tweaks (ip adress range & ports)
  • no local debug experience
  • additional latency
  • on premise service can be a single point of failure
  • for pub sub & guaranteed delivery embrace queues and topics with relay
